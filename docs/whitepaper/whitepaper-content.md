## Abstract  
XFUEL is a decentralized yield rail that routes Theta EdgeCloud revenue—amplified by the TDROP 2.0 AI agent economy—into auto-compounding Cosmos liquid staked tokens (LSTs). Launched with a live product (v1.0.0), XFUEL delivers sub-4s swaps, institutional-grade tools, and a cyberpunk interface for Edge Node operators, creators, and yield seekers.

This whitepaper outlines XFUEL's architecture, groundbreaking tokenomics, and vision for a self-evolving ecosystem where protocol revenue drives perpetual innovation, deflation, and holder equity in spin-out projects.
